Job scheduling without the whiteboard

Assign jobs to operatives, track progress in real time, and give your field team a clean mobile view of what they need to do today. From a quote accepted to a job scheduled in two clicks.

  • Assign to any operative or team
  • Mobile-first view for field staff

Simple to get started

Up and running in 30 minutes.

Lustre is designed so you can get to your first real quote in a single session — no lengthy setup, no training course required.

1

Set up your business profile

Add your logo, business name, address, and service types. This takes under 5 minutes and powers every quote and PDF Lustre generates for you.

2

Add your first client and create a quote

Add a real client, build a quote with your line items and pricing, and send it — Lustre generates a branded PDF and a shareable link automatically. Your client gets a professional proposal, not an email attachment.

3

Convert to a job and assign to your team

When the quote is accepted, turn it into a scheduled job in two clicks. Assign to an operative, set the date, and your team sees it immediately on their mobile view. Your client never needs to be told again.
